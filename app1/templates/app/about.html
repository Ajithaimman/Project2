"Hunger is not a problem. It is an obscenity. How wonderful it is that nobody need wait a single moment before starting to improve the world". – Anne Frank

No Food Waste is an organisation which aims to redistribute excess food from weddings, parties, events to those who are hungry. The idea took root in Coimbatore, where we started with 2 Shopper Bags and a volunteer to collect the surplus food and deliver to the homeless through Public Transportation System. We took an oath on that day to stop food waste and satisfy the hunger of many, in a quest to make the “World Hunger Free.”
